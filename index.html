About Me

Hi, I'm Andrew and I'm a Junior Electrical Engineering major at The Cooper Union, pursuing a double minor in Math and CS.

I’m passionate about optimization and stochastic processes— especially applying mathematical tools to tackle real-world challenges. After graduating from Cooper Union, I plan to pursue graduate studies in Applied Mathematics. This Summer, I will be at Cal Poly Pomona working with Dr. Jimmy Risk in Kernel Engineering with GPs and SVMs.

Outside of academia, I am a semi-professional badminton player. I'm a former member of the US Junior National Team and previously competed at the 2022 World Junior Championships. In July, I'll be competing in Germany at the 2025 FISU World University Games. Go Team USA!

Projects:

  • CooperWall: A full-stack web application built with Spring Boot, PostgreSQL, and REACT frontend that lets the Cooper community share memorable quotes. Includes Firebase Authentification!
  • ScopeTV: Real-time conversion of analog NTSC video signals into X-Y signals for display on a standard laboratory oscilloscope, emulating a vintage-style monochrome television.
  • Color Organ w/ Audio Amplifier: LED display that visualizes music by mapping bass, mids, and treble to different colors. Comes with volume control as well!
  • Playable Theremin: Theremin that uses analog LC oscillators whose frequency changes with hand-induced capacitance, allowing real-time pitch control based on proximity.
