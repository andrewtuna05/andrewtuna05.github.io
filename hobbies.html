My Hobbies

Badminton Player

As time-consuming as engineering can be, I like to be away from schoolwork and do activities that I enjoy.

For the past decade, I've been a professional badminton player and while I'm semi-retired now, I still occasionally compete in tournaments.

Some of My Achievements:

  • Highest USA Junior Rank: #1
  • 2025 World University Games and 2022 World Junior Championships USA Team Member
  • 5x Pan American Junior Team Member and Pan American Junior Champion
  • US Adult National Runner-Up and Junior National Champion

I've traveled the world to train and compete in tournaments. Some countries I've been to include: Canada, Mexico, Guatemala, Peru, Brazil, Spain, China, and Taiwan.

This summer, I will be traveling to Germany to compete at the 2025 World University Games!
